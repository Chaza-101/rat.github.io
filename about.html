Who are We?

Welcome to the Chicago Underground Rat Investigation Society. I'm Gerald, a seasoned pest control worker on a mission to uncover the truth about a rumored giant rat living beneath the streets of Chicago.

I've heard the stories for years, and while I'm getting older and a bit tired, I can't let this mystery go unsolved. I'm willing to do the work, but I need help. If you've got the curiosity and the grit to dive into the city's underground with me, we might just find the answers.

Join me in solving this rodent riddle. Together, we'll get to the bottom of it.
